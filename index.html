Welcome

This website grew out of a simple idea: bringing live performances and public lectures from across the Five Colleges into one easy-to-use calendar. Amherst, Hampshire, Mount Holyoke, Smith, and UMass offer a wealth of cultural events, many of them free or inexpensive. Browse around, see what appeals to you, and check back — we’ll keep updating the calendar throughout the year.

-- Bernie Davidow

Note on Listings

We note which events are free and include ticket prices whenever possible. If you click “Details,” you’ll be taken directly to the college’s event page for the latest info — including last-minute changes, cancellations, and anything new that’s been added.

This site is not affiliated with any of the colleges or event organizers.

Have a Question or Suggestion?

If you spot an event we missed, notice an error, or just want to share feedback, I’d love to hear from you. .
